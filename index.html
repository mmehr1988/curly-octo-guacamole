About

Salam and welcome! My name is Mehdi Mehrabani and I’m one half of a musical group Saint Abdullah. In 2017, my brother and I began writing music to serve as cultural translators,with the goal of challenging stereotypes and acting as a conduit between unnecessary enemies.


We set sail on a journey with no destination in mind other than self-discovery. With no musical background, this journey seemed impossible at first, yet we understood the importance of failing and how it would be pivotal to our growth.


I believe music and coding are like two siblings. They have their differences, but their genes are from the same parents. I’m fortunate to have the opportunity to pursue a career in Web Development, and my hope is to help you achieve your goals.
